Return & Refund Policy

Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not satisfied, you may return the product according to the terms outlined below:

  • Products can be returned within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  • The item must be in its original condition, unused, and with all tags attached.
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part.

How to Return an Item

If you would like to return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us via our Contact Us page or email with your order number and details of the item you're returning.
  2. We will send you a return authorization along with instructions for sending the product back.
  3. Once the product is received and inspected, we will issue a refund or replacement depending on your preference.

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days once your return is approved.

Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., defective items).

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our return policy, feel free to reach out to us. We're here to help!
