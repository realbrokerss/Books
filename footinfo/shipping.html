Shipping Policy

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed after 5:00 PM will be processed on the next business day.

Note: Orders placed on weekends or public holidays may experience a slight delay in processing.

Shipping Methods

We offer a variety of shipping methods to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery in 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivery in 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivery by the next business day (if ordered before 12 PM).

Shipping times may vary depending on your location. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number.

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order. You can view your shipping costs at checkout.

  • Free shipping on orders over ₹500 within the domestic region.
  • Flat-rate shipping of ₹99 for orders under ₹500.

International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination. Additional customs fees may apply.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment on the courier's website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need further assistance, please contact us.
